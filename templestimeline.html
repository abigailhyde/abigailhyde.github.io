Temples Timeline

The Temples Timeline app was featured in the October 2021 edition of BYU Studies Quarterly. The app is available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Temples Timeline displays a chronology of temples built by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from 1836 to the present day in a spiral layout, suitable for handheld touchscreen devices. I worked on this with Tsz Chin Lam, Litian Zhang, Grace Xie, and our professor Dr. Geoff Draper.
